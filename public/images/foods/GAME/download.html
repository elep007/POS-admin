You've come to the right place for studio recording equipment - Sweetwater began as a recording studio, and here you'll find the latest professional audio gear plus expertise you can count on. We stake our reputation on it - from expert pre-sales advice to our free 2-year Total Confidence Coverage warranty, our goal is to help you get the right gear your studio needs to prosper.

Are you just starting out and equipping your first studio? Whether you're designing a new Pro Tools recording system, choosing microphones for specific applications, upgrading your studio monitors and acoustic treatment, or anything else studio related, we're here to help. Whether you want to record full bands or do simple voice-over work, you can trust Sweetwater to help you find exactly what you need while avoiding potential pitfalls.

Do you want to upgrade the sound in your studio? Consider adding some new microphones, investing in acoustic treatment, and getting better monitor speakers or headphones. Looking for better tools to craft your mixes? We offer a wide range of world-class signal processors in a variety of formats including desktop units, rackmount items, and 500 Series processors.

Many of us at Sweetwater produce, record, and perform our own music, so we know the kind of challenges you face as an audio professional. From choosing microphone preamps and processors for specific recording applications to the furniture you use to arrange and organize your studio, right down to the cables, direct boxes and other essential audio tools you need in your equipment bag, we can help you achieve superior sound in any application.

Do you want to expand into video production? We can help you there too, with premium cameras, cutting-edge video mixers, and video editing software. Are you an experienced engineer running a multi-room facility with audio, video, and multimedia production? We serve many of the top studios in the world, and we know how crucial your sessions are. With fast, free shipping, personalized professional advice, and industry-leading post-sales support, we're eager to help you achieve your goals and grow your studio's creative potential.
